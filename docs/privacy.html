Introduction

MedResus ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application MedResus (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected during login for user identification and license activation
  • Name: Collected for user profile and event attribution in case logs
  • Hospital/Unit Information: Session location data (hospital name, department/unit)

Medical/Health Data

  • Resuscitation Case Logs: Timestamps, event descriptions, interventions performed
  • Patient Location: Bed/room location (optional)
  • UHID: Unique hospital identifier (optional, if provided by user)
  • Quality Metrics: Calculated from case events (time to interventions, CPR quality)

Technical Data

  • Device Information: Operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, session duration
  • License Information: Subscription tier, activation date, license key

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core app functionality (case logging, documentation)
  • Enable license activation and subscription management
  • Generate quality metrics and debrief summaries
  • Allow CSV export of case data for audit and research
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Communicate important updates about licenses or subscriptions

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All case data is stored locally on your device using encrypted AsyncStorage
  • No cloud sync: We do NOT upload your case logs or patient data to our servers
  • Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app

Security Measures

  • Device-level encryption for stored data
  • Email-based license validation
  • No transmission of patient identifiable information
  • Secure local storage using React Native AsyncStorage

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Share Your Data

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or medical data with third parties
  • Case logs remain on your device only
  • Email addresses are used solely for license activation

Email Export Feature

  • You may choose to export case data via email using your device's email client
  • This is a manual action initiated by you
  • We do not have access to exported data sent via email

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

Data Retention

  • Case Data: Retained on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
  • User Profile: Stored locally until you log out or uninstall
  • License Information: Stored locally for subscription validation
  • No Server-Side Storage: We do not retain any data on our servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app at any time
  • Export: Export your case logs as CSV files
  • Delete: Clear individual cases or all data from the app
  • Uninstall: Remove the app and all associated data from your device

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Within the app: Navigate to cases and delete individual entries
  2. Complete removal: Uninstall the MedResus app from your device
  3. Contact us: Email snmandal1953@gmail.com to request assistance

HIPAA Compliance (US Users)

Important Notice for US Healthcare Providers:

  • MedResus is designed to minimize Protected Health Information (PHI) collection
  • The app does NOT require patient names, medical record numbers, or other direct identifiers
  • All data is stored locally on your device, not on cloud servers
  • You are responsible for ensuring your use complies with HIPAA requirements at your institution
  • We recommend using only de-identified data or institution-approved patient identifiers

MedResus is NOT a HIPAA Business Associate as we do not access, store, or transmit your data on our servers.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability (CSV export)
  • Right to withdraw consent
